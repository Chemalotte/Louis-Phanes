ARTWORK |

My art practice has been a constant source of enjoyment
in my life, when I was younger my mum would spend
rainy days with me drawing (poorly) offering me my
greatest gift to go on with. As I got older and I was left
to create alone, I would be encapsulated for hours, these
moments have been the only true constant that have
travelled with me.

I am inspired by my lived experience and surrealist artists, I use my time to attemtpt to bring colour and fun into every aspect of my artwork. By using bright colours and playful imagery I am able to reconcile difficult aspects of my past and present. Currently I am learning how to become a full stack developer, I intend to include this into my current work and created this landing page from the information I have been able to gather over the last 30 days. Although I was already learning to code the idea that the skills could be used in my current practice was sparked by artwork in the current GOMA collection.
